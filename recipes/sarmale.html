Sarmale

Description

It's a dish made out of minced meat and onions in cabbage rolls

It's delicious as heck

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Minced meat
  • Condiments
  • Cabbage leaves

Steps

  1. Cook the meat
  2. Boil the rice
  3. Put your condiments on the mix
  4. Roll the mix in cabbage leaves
